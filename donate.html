Expand software opportunity

Help CodeSquad expand software opportunity

CodeSquad was born from the realization that Greater Boston's booming tech economy was leaving behind our local communities of color. High-tech districts like Kendall Square in Cambridge have low unemployment, high average salaries and excellent job security. But the picture is very different in Nubian Square in Roxbury, or in neighborhoods like Dorchester, Mattapan or Hyde Park. CodeSquad seeks to build a bridge from Boston's urban core to its technology job market, and develop a new pipeline of software talent for area employers.

CodeSquad has received the Platinum Seal of Transparency from GuideStar.

CodeSquad was named to MassTLC's Tech Top 50 for Community Impact.


How to help

You can help CodeSquad in several ways: directly with a credit card or bank (ACH) transfer, through a Donor-Advised Fund, and matching gifts through your employer.
