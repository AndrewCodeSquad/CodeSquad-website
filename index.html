CodeSquad expands software opportunity
Our mission is to build careers and energize the talent pipeline

CodeSquad is a non-profit organization that trains talented adults from Boston's urban core to become full-stack software developers and launch a new career. See what you can learn, how to expand your workforce, or how to help.

Why partner with CodeSquad?

New talent

Grads from our bootcamp don't match the typical demographics of the US tech sector:

  • 86% people of color
  • 64% women
  • 10% non-binary
Strong support

CodeSquad has increased its institutional support and added new funders every year for the past 5 years.

Industry interest

More than 60 developers, technology execs and supporters attended CodeSquad's 2025 Project Night. It was a chance to meet our Graduates and watch them demo their work.

100+ Engineers & Execs

Over 100 professionals have volunteered as coding coaches, career panelists and advisors.
