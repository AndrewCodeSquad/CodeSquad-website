CodeSquad is hiring for the following positions:


Technology Job Placement Specialist

We are looking for a recruiter, a relationship builder and a coach who is enthusiastic about technology and familiar with the career pathways of web development. Our goal is to improve the tech-talent sourcing process by placing talented individuals who don't fit the stereotype of a computer programmer into great software jobs. Email resume & cover letter to jobs@lenaparkcdc.org.


Development/Fundraising Consultant

We are seeking a fundraising professional familiar with the STEM and high-tech ecosystem of Boston to help grow our organization and increase its impact. Compensation is competitive, at an hourly rate, based on experience. Experience with corporate, foundation and government grants preferred. Email resume & cover letter to jobs@lenaparkcdc.org.
