Congratulations to our 2026 Cohort!

We are delighted to announce the graduation of CodeSquad’s 2026 Cohort. After months of learning, building, and testing, these graduates have developed the technical skills and confidence to begin careers in software. Their dedication reflects what's possible when talent and determination meet mentorship and opportunity.

Thank you for attending Project Night

Project Night was an incredible showcase of everything our 2026 Cohort has accomplished. Graduates presented web apps to help find pets to adopt, improve personal relationships, analyze real estate investments and plan DIY projects, all using AI to process user input. To everyone who attended, encouraged, and supported our mission: thank you for believing in the next generation of software developers. The future is bright, and this is just the beginning!
