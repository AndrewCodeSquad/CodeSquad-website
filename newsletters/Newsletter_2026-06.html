Artificial Intelligence training begins at CodeSquad


CodeSquad students spent the last two weeks learning AI engineering — not just prompting, but how to build production-level agent systems. Students designed AI agents to integrate with external tools like Slack and perform real-world tasks. They also used vector databases and Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) to gather accurate, data-driven responses, culminating in projects powered by their own datasets. Congrats to our first trainees in AI!
